# Privacy Policy — Démineur

_Last updated: 2026._

Démineur is a free, open-source Minesweeper game. It does not show ads, does not
sell anything, and does not use any analytics or tracking SDK.

## Data collected

Démineur itself collects nothing and sends nothing to any server. The only data
it stores is your best completion time per difficulty, kept on your device only
via Android's DataStore.

If Android Auto Backup is enabled on your device, this local data is included in
your backup like any other app data — it never leaves Google's standard backup
pipeline and is not accessible to us.

## Optional Google Play Games sign-in

Démineur can optionally submit your best times to Google Play Games Services
leaderboards, if you choose to sign in. This is never required to play the game.
When you do sign in, your gameplay times and your Google Play Games profile
identifier are shared with Google under Google's own privacy policy:
<https://policies.google.com/privacy>. Declining or skipping sign-in keeps
everything local, with no loss of functionality beyond the online leaderboard.

## Permissions

The app requests no permissions beyond what Android grants by default (Play
Games sign-in, when used, is handled entirely by Google Play services).

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised as an issue on the project's GitHub
repository: <https://github.com/gghez/le-demineur>.
